Young Adults

A community for parishioners in their late teens, twenties and thirties — faith, friendship, and a fair bit of tea and biscuits afterwards.

Weekly

Youth Holy Hour

Every Tuesday, 7:30–8:30 PM. Quiet Eucharistic adoration with music, open to all young adults — no experience of prayer needed, just come as you are.

Monthly

Young Adult Mass

Last Sunday of the month, part of our regular 11 AM Mass, followed by tea, coffee and a chance to meet others in the group afterwards.
